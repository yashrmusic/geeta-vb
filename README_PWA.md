# PWA Installation Guide

This app can be installed on iOS and Android devices as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

## For Android/Chrome Users:
1. Visit the website on your Android device
2. Look for the "Install" button in the header, or
3. Tap the menu (3 dots) in Chrome and select "Add to Home screen"
4. The app will install and appear on your home screen like a native app

## For iOS/Safari Users:
1. Visit the website on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow) at the bottom
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" in the top right
5. The app will appear on your home screen

## Creating App Icons

You'll need to create two icon files:
- `public/icon-192.png` (192x192 pixels)
- `public/icon-512.png` (512x512 pixels)

You can use any image editing tool to create these. The icons will appear on the home screen when installed.
